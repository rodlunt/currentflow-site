FAQ

Common questions about CurrentFlow.

Everything you need to know about the platform and when it's available.

What is CurrentFlow?

CurrentFlow is solar business management software built for Australian solar installers. It combines quoting, CRM, job scheduling, project management, and STC entitlement tracking in one connected platform, so every job moves from first contact to lodged certificate without re-entering data or switching between tools.

How does STC tracking work in CurrentFlow?

Every job in CurrentFlow tracks its Small-scale Technology Certificate (STC) entitlements automatically. You can see at a glance how many jobs are sitting between installed and certificates lodged, which shows you exactly where your working capital is tied up.

Is CurrentFlow only for Australian solar businesses?

Yes. CurrentFlow is purpose-built for the Australian market and the STC scheme. It handles the compliance, cashflow, and operational requirements specific to running a solar install business in Australia.
