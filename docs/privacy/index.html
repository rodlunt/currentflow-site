Privacy Policy

CurrentFlow (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store personal information.

What we collect

Waitlist sign-ups: When you submit your email address on currentflow.com.au, we store that email address to notify you when early access opens. We do not share it with third parties.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. This data is aggregated and does not identify individuals. You can opt out via your browser’s privacy settings or a content-blocking extension.

Contact enquiries: If you email us at hello@currentflow.com.au, we retain that correspondence to respond to your enquiry.

How we use your information

  • To notify waitlist subscribers when early access opens
  • To respond to enquiries
  • To understand site usage through aggregated analytics

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to any third party.

Data storage

Waitlist emails are stored in a Google spreadsheet accessible only to CurrentFlow. We do not use third-party email marketing platforms at this stage.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@currentflow.com.au.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product develops. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
