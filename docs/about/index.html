About CurrentFlow

CurrentFlow is an all-in-one platform for Australian solar install businesses — quoting, CRM, scheduling, project management, and STC compliance in a single connected workflow.

Who built it

My name is Rodney Lunt. I have spent more than 20 years working in the Australian solar industry, on both the install side and in wholesale supply. Over that time I watched good businesses lose sales to bad processes — leads dropping through the cracks, STC forms filed late, jobs entered into five different tools that never talked to each other.

CurrentFlow is the platform I always wanted and could never find. I am building it to fix that.

What problem it solves

Most Australian solar installers run their business across three to five separate subscriptions that do not connect: a quoting tool, a CRM, a scheduling app, a project management platform, and something for STCs. The same job details get entered again and again. Follow-up slips when it gets busy. A mistimed STC submission can tie up real working capital for months.

CurrentFlow brings all of it into one system. Enter a job once and carry it from lead to quote to install to invoice — no re-entry, no gaps, no juggling logins.
